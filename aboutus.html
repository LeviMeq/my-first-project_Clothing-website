About Us

Company

Solde is one of the largest international fashion companies. It belongs to Indotec, one of the world’s largest distribution groups.

The customer is at the heart of our unique business model, which includes design, production, distribution and sales through our extensive retail network.

Under our UES DoTEc (economic and social unit of Indotec) standards in France and in accordance with the new applicable regulations in France, the 2019 index score used to measure professional equality between women and men within the company stands at 98 points out of 100.
